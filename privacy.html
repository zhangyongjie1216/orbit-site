Orbit Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Orbit is an independent app for searching public App Store information and comparing regional app prices.

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. Orbit does not require an account and does not use advertising or tracking technologies.

Orbit stores your selected and favorite storefronts locally on your device using iOS system storage. This data is not sent to us.

To provide search, price comparison, and currency conversion features, Orbit sends requests to Apple iTunes Search/Lookup services and a public currency exchange rate service. These services may receive technical information such as your IP address as part of normal network requests.

If you contact us by email, we will use your email address only to respond to your message.

Orbit 隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 6 月 5 日

Orbit 是一个用于查询公开 App Store 信息并比较不同地区 App 价格的独立工具。

我们不收集、出售或共享个人信息。Orbit 不需要账号，不使用广告或追踪技术。
