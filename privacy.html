Orbit Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Orbit is an independent utility for searching public App Store information and comparing app prices across regions.

Orbit does not require an account, collect personal information, use advertising, or use tracking technologies.

The app stores your selected and favorite storefronts locally on your device. This information is not sent to us.

To provide search, price comparison, and currency conversion features, Orbit sends requests to Apple's public iTunes Search and Lookup services and to a public exchange-rate service. These services may process technical information, such as your IP address, as part of handling the request.

If you contact us by email, we use your email address only to respond to your message.

Orbit 隐私政策

更新日期：2026 年 6 月 6 日

Orbit 是一个用于查询公开 App Store 信息并比较不同地区 App 价格的独立工具。

Orbit 不需要账号，不收集个人信息，不使用广告，也不使用追踪技术。

App 会在你的设备本地保存已选择和收藏的 App Store 地区。这些信息不会发送给我们。

为了提供搜索、价格比较和汇率换算功能，Orbit 会向 Apple 公开的 iTunes Search 和 Lookup 服务以及公开汇率服务发送请求。这些服务可能会在处理请求时接收 IP 地址等技术信息。

如果你通过电子邮件联系我们，我们只会使用你的邮箱地址回复你的消息。
